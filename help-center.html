Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions

How do I track my order?
You can track your order by visiting our Track Order page and entering your order number. You'll also receive email updates with tracking information once your order ships.
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery takes 1-2 business days. International delivery takes 7-14 business days. Delivery times may vary based on location and carrier.
Can I cancel my order?
Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of placement. Please contact our support team or the seller directly for assistance with cancellations. Once shipped, orders cannot be canceled.
What if I receive the wrong item?
If you receive the wrong item, please contact the seller immediately. Provide your order number and photos of the incorrect item. The seller will arrange for a replacement or refund.
Do you offer free shipping?
Yes! We offer free shipping on all orders over $250 within the continental US. Standard shipping is $15 for orders under $250.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to select countries worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on destination. Customs fees and duties are the buyer's responsibility.
Can I change my shipping address after ordering?
If your order hasn't shipped yet, please contact us immediately to update the shipping address. Once shipped, we cannot change the address. You may need to contact the carrier directly.
What is your return policy?
Returns are handled by individual sellers. Most sellers offer a 30-day return policy. Items must be in original condition with packaging. Please contact the seller directly for return requests.
How do I return a damaged item?
Report damaged items within 48 hours of delivery. Provide photos of the damage and your order number. The seller will arrange for a return and replacement or refund.
How do I create an account?
You can create an account by clicking the Register button at the top of the page. Fill out the form with your details and verify your email to get started.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Click the "Forgot Password?" link on the Login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions to create a new password.
How do I update my account information?
Log in to your account and go to the Account page. From there, you can update your personal information, shipping addresses, and email preferences.
How do I become a seller?
You can apply to become a seller by visiting our Become a Seller page. Fill out the application with your business details and our team will review it within 48 hours.
How much does it cost to sell?
We offer flexible pricing plans for sellers. The Starter plan is free with basic features. The Professional plan is $29/month with advanced features. Visit our Pricing page for details.
How do I manage my products?
Once approved as a seller, you can manage your products from the Seller Dashboard. You can add, edit, and delete products, track orders, and view analytics.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for bulk orders. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes! We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) to protect your payment information. We never store your full payment details on our servers. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways.
